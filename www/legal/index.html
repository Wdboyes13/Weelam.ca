Privacy Policy

weelam.ca, and any sub-domains of it (together, the "website") do not collect any user data, except for that outlined by this following document.
All re-directs to services not part of the wesite are neither with unaffiliated nor endorsed by the website, and are governed by the Privacy Policy of that website
All parts of weelam.ca use CloudFlare Analytics. CloudFlare Analytics anonymously shares the following data with the owner:
  • Content Type Requested (JSON, JavaScript, HTML, etc.)
  • SSL Protocol Type (TLSv1.3, TLSv1.2, etc.)
  • What errors you encountered during your visit and the number of them
  • Which requests where served through cached data
  • The amount of encrypted requests, and encrypted bandwidth during your visit
  • The amount of bandwidth used during your visit
  • The country your request originated from
  • If you have visited the website(s) before
  • Your Internet Protocol (IP) Address (We do not use this to personally identify users, and is only reviewed in the event of a potential attack)

The collected data is used for the following purposes
  • Monitoring potential DDoS/DoS attacks
  • Monitoring any potential bugs/broken parts on the website
  • Monitoring website success
For details on how CloudFlare handles analytics data please take the time to review their own Privacy Policy at https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/
